Title: Support

----

Text:

Q: Is a credit card necessary to sign up for a free account?
A: No. No credit card is necessary.

Q: Is there a time limit for a free account?
A: No. You may use your free account forever.

Q: Can I subscribe after creating a free account?
A: Yes, you can subscribe anytime you want to, and cancel anytime, then resubscribe, etc.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: Credit and debit cards of the following brands: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, JCB, and Diners Club.

Q: How secure is making payments?
A: All payment information is encrypted by and processed through (link: https://stripe.com/about text: Stripe class: btn).

Q: Which browsers are supported?
A: Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Due to ruby (furigana) display issues, Internet Explorer is not supported at this time. 

If you have any questions or concerns, or trouble using the site, please email me: stephen[at]languageinmotion.jp and I will get back to you as soon as possible.

# Tips

- After you pull up a definition, click anywhere outside of it to close it.
- For additional word research, try out the free online Japanese dictionary (link: http://www.weblio.jp/ text: Weblio class: btn).

# If you find editorial errors...

Every book has been reviewed multiple times over to ensure each word has an accurate definition. However, I am bound to have slipped up on some. If you find any such editorial errors, let me know and I will try to fix them as soon as possible.
